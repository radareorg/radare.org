# Advent Of Radare

Throughout December, we'll publish daily posts featuring tips and tricks for using radare2. Each post will explore different aspects of the tool, allowing you to learn, practice, and share your solutions. Join the community discussion and contribute to the project using the <a href="">#aor24</a> hashtag!
